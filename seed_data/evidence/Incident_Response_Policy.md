# Incident Response Policy

## Documented Incident Response Plan
AcmeCloud maintains a documented incident response plan that defines roles, escalation paths, investigation procedures, communication expectations, and post-incident follow-up requirements for security events.

## Incident Triage Timeline
AcmeCloud triages reported or detected security incidents internally within 24 hours so severity, ownership, and immediate containment actions can be established promptly.

## Tabletop Exercises
AcmeCloud performs incident response tabletop exercises at least annually to validate response procedures, decision-making paths, and coordination between engineering, security, and leadership stakeholders.

## Customer Notification Commitments
When a confirmed security incident requires external notice, AcmeCloud notifies affected customers without undue delay and according to applicable legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations. This policy does not define a universal commitment to notify all customers within 72 hours for every confirmed breach scenario.
