# Access Control Policy

## Administrative Access
AcmeCloud requires multifactor authentication (MFA) for all administrative access to production systems, cloud management consoles, identity providers, and other privileged infrastructure.

## Role-Based Access Control
AcmeCloud uses role-based access control (RBAC) for internal systems. Access is granted through defined roles that align to job responsibilities, and privileged roles are limited to authorized administrators.

## Access Reviews
AcmeCloud performs user access reviews at least quarterly for production systems and other internal systems that store or manage sensitive company or customer data.

## Production Access Restrictions
Production access is restricted to authorized personnel only. Access to production environments is approved based on business need and limited to trained engineering, security, or platform personnel with assigned responsibilities.

## Single Sign-On Scope
AcmeCloud uses single sign-on (SSO) for core workforce systems such as email, source control, and ticketing. SSO is not currently enforced for all workforce applications, so additional applications may use other approved authentication methods.
